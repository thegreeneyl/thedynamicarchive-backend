ABOUT:
This module extend the functionality of video embed field for soundcloud.


STEPS:
1. First download video embed field and place it in contrib folder in sites/all/modules/ folder and enable this module.
2. After that download video embed soundcloud module (video_embed_soundcloud), and place it in contrib folder in sites/all/modules folder and enable this module.
3. Create a field in your content type of type video embed and widget as video.
4. After that a field will appear in your content type.
5. Just place ustream video url, of a video in that field.
6. Video will appear in your page of soundcloud.
